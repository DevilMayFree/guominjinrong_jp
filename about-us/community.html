    How We Help The Community

    Citizens Commitment

    Giving back is in our Credo. At Citizens we are committed to strengthening our communities to build a more inclusive and sustainable future for all those we serve. With a deep understanding of our diverse communities, we work alongside our partners to financially empower our neighbors, strengthen our communities through workforce development, and mobilize our colleagues to share their time and talent to make a difference.

    Video

    Creating a Brighter Tomorrow

    Our commitment to continual environmental, social and governance (ESG) progress is woven into the fabric of our business, as we work to create a thriving, sustainable, inclusive future for all of our stakeholders.

    Learn more about our commitment to responsible citizenship and read our latest Corporate Responsibility Report.

    Corporate Giving

    Citizens is committed to supporting organizations focused on Financial Empowerment and Workforce Development where our grants can have a measurable impact on the community.

    Learn more about our corporate giving and how to apply.

    Financial Empowerment

    Working with local nonprofits, we support educational programs that help adults and children develop healthy financial habits. Citizens also partners with Junior Achievement (JA), the nation’s largest organization dedicated to giving young people the knowledge and skills they need to own their economic success, plan for their futures, and make smart academic and economic choices.

    Learn more about our Financial Empowerment program.

    Workforce Development

    We help communities across our footprint build the workforce of the future through our support of job training, career education and career coaching programs.

    We also help address social challenges through our Champions In Action program.

    Learn more about our efforts to strengthen communities.

    Colleague Engagement

    We enable and equip our colleagues to make a difference by sharing their time, talent and resources. Through our efforts, we strive to help people thrive, small businesses succeed and communities flourish. Learn more about how our colleagues are making an impact in their communities.
