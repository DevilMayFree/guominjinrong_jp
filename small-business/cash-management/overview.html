    Additional Cash Management Solutions

    Contact us to get more information on these products to help keep your business operations running smoothly.

    Exchange Financial Data: accessXCHANGE®

    A safe, secure and compatible format for transmitting and receiving receivables and payment data to and from the bank.

    Escrow Management: accessESCROW®

    This robust online tool empowers you to simplify the administration of your escrow accounts while ensuring total privacy and security.

    Liquidity Control: accessLIQUIDITY®

    A comprehensive dashboard view of your business’ liquidity positions with detailed, point-in-time information and conduct market research.

    Zero Balance Accounts (ZBAs)

    Automatically consolidate funds into one account that can then be used to fund disbursements or investments.

    Remote Check Deposit: accessDEPOSIT®

    A safe, secure way to capture and transmit digital images of checks right from your place of business.

    International Trade: accessGlobal Trade Flow®

    Manage trade finance transactions in real time, providing direct, 24/7 connectivity throughout the lifecycle of the transaction.

    Simplify Receivables: accessLOCKBOX®

    Reduce the potential for theft and fraud for checks, money orders and other paper-based remittances.

    Secure ACH: ACHieve Access®

    Take greater control over ACH transactions and safeguard your accounts against fraud.

    With the right tools, efficient cash flow management can be effortless. Our comprehensive suite of electronic and paper-based collection services make cash management fast, easy, and accurate—so you can focus on moving your business forward.
