    Branches & ATMs

    Visit us at over 1,000 branches at 3,000 ATMs.

    Online and mobile banking

    Save your business time, money and trips to the bank with our online and mobile banking services4.

    Overdraft protection

    Take control of liquidity on your accounts with our Business Overdraft Plans, including overdraft line of credit1 and no fee savings overdraft transfers.

    Plus, with Business Overdraft Pass® no Overdraft Fee will be charged on any overdraft item of $5 or less or when your account is overdrawn and the total overdrawn amount at the end of the same business day is $5 or less. Business Advisor Checking has coverage of $10 or less.5

    Invoicing and accounting

    Get paid faster. Invoice, track and accept payments6 without moving funds from other apps - you can get paid directly into your business checking account.

    Plus, you can also simplify your accounting by automating bookkeeping entries and access financial reports in online banking. Add for only $9.99/month.7

    Business Checking Frequently Asked Questions

    • Citizens offers a couple of ways for you to begin the process of applying for a business banking account. You can apply online or schedule an appointment.
      • Online: Utilize our comparison page. This can help you identify which accounts are best suited to your needs and goals.
      • By Phone: Speak to a Business Banking Specialist – Monday through Friday, 7:00 a.m. to 7:00 p.m. ET and Saturdays, 9:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. EST. Call 1-888-678-7100.
      • At the Branch: Speak to a dedicated Business Banking Specialist who can answer your questions and review your needs. Find a branch near you.
      • At Your Business: Set up an appointment to have a Business Banking Specialist come to your business and review the best products for your needs. Call 1-888-678-7100.
    • Depending on the type of your business, you may need different materials to open your account. Visit our opening a business account checklist to get started.
    • You can begin using Online Banking immediately after signing up for your business banking account.

    • We have partnered with Deluxe to provide safe and convenient options to order personal checks, Home Equity Line of Credit checks, or Personal Line of Credit checks:

      • By phone: 1-800-231-9915
      • Online: Deluxe Business Checks & Solutions (order directly with Deluxe, our preferred check provider)
      • Online Banking: Log In | Enroll (order checks through the Business Accounts Services section)
      • In person: Find A Branch

      When you place your order using any of these convenient options, be sure to select your check style and confirm the following:

      • Name and Address
      • Routing Number
      • Account Number
      • Check Starting Number

      Please note: If you would like to change a name or address on your checks, please visit a branch near you or call us at 1-800-862-6200 to complete your order.

      Your checks will be sent in a new, flat package designed by Deluxe to accommodate new postal automation and keep delivery costs to a minimum. The new packaging is a streamlined blue envelope that is ¾ of an inch thick.

      Inside you will find a flat, easy-to-assemble box where you can store your checks as you did with the former packaging. Be sure to organize your checks in sequential order. The new packaging keeps delivery costs low while maintaining the security and quality we’re committed to providing.

    Have more questions? Get more answers in our full FAQs section.
