    Overdraft coverage

    Trouble proof your liquidity with one of our overdraft plans for business banking. Rest easy, knowing that available funds can be automatically transferred to your business checking account in the event of an overdraft.

    Our Overdraft Plans for business banking give you a cushion to prevent you from bouncing checks or being penalized for overdrafts. Available funds can be automatically transferred to your business checking account in the event of an overdraft, giving you the peace of mind you need to operate your business. Explore our options for overdraft coverage below.

    Overdraft Line of Credit5

    With an Overdraft Line of Credit your checking account is linked to your overdraft line of credit. Funds are automatically transferred from your line of credit to cover your ATM withdrawals, debit card purchases, checks, and online bill payments when you don't have the necessary available balance in your checking account.

    Note: Your line of credit may not be large enough to cover all withdrawals that have overdrawn your checking account that day. Once any single transaction exceeds your available line of credit, no additional transactions will be covered, even if there are available funds in your line of credit. Any transaction(s) in the checking account that cannot be covered by your line of credit will cause your account to become overdrawn.

    Savings Overdraft Transfer

    With Savings Overdraft Transfer, your checking account is linked to your savings account. Funds are automatically transferred from your savings account to cover your ATM withdrawals, debit card purchases, checks, and online bill payments when you don't have the necessary available balance in your checking account.

    Note: To use Savings Overdraft Transfer, you must have sufficient available funds in your linked savings account to cover all withdrawals that have overdrawn your checking account that day. If you do not, we will not transfer any funds. Items in the checking account not covered will cause your account to become overdrawn.
